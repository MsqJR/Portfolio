## Brand Personality
- **Voice/Tone**: Rigorous, authoritative, precise, and highly technical.
- **3-Word Personality**: *Rigorous, Stable, Decoupled.*
- **Visual Feel**: Terminal-inspired Gruvbox aesthetic—structured, warm dark/light tones, using JetBrains Mono for system elements, and layout precision.

## Anti-references
- Saturated modern SaaS landing pages with huge purple-to-blue gradient text and generic illustrations.
- "Ghost cards" combining thin borders and soft, massive drop shadows.
- Overly rounded corners (border-radius > 16px) on cards or buttons.
- Flashy, non-functional animations that distract from technical content.

## Design Principles
- **Decoupled Grid Structure**: UI sections must follow a clear, predictable layout structure with solid, distinct border lines (e.g. 1px borders using Gruvbox values) rather than floating cards.
- **Terminal-Grade Spacing**: Typography and margins should feel clean and console-like, prioritizing monospace styling and code-block readouts for specs.
- **Production Contrast**: Ensure high legibility at all times. Colors must align strictly with Gruvbox dark/light values with contrast ratios exceeding 4.5:1.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Strict contrast targets for body, placeholder, and status indicator text.
- Comprehensive support for users with `prefers-reduced-motion` settings.
